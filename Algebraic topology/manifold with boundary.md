**Theorem.** For any odd dimensional manifold with boundary $X$, the inclusion $i: \partial X \to X$ kills half the homology.

**proof.** $H^n(X) \xrightarrow{i_*} H^n(\partial X) \xrightarrow{\delta} H^{n+1}(X,\partial X)$
