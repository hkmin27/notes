### The strand algebra

---
**Definition** The strand algebra $\mathcal{A}(n,k)$ is a free $\mathbb{F}$-module with generators $\mu=(S,T,\phi)$ where $S,T \subset \{1,...,n\}$ and $\phi:S \to T$ is a non-decreasing bijection. Let $\text{Inv}(\mu)$ be the set of inversions of $\phi$, i.e. pairs $i > j$ in $S$ such that $\phi(i) < \phi (j)$.
