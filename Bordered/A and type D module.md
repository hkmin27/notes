### $A_\infty$-modules
$A_\infty$-algebra over $\mathbb{F}$ is a pair $\mathcal{A}=(A,\{\mu_i\})$ where $A$ is a graded $\mathbb{F}$-module and the $\mu_i$ are a sequence of multiplication maps

$$\mu_i:A^{\otimes i} \to A[2-i]$$

for $i 	= 1, 2, ...$
