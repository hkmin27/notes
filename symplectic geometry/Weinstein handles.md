### Weinstein handles

We will define a *Weinstein k-handle* as a subset of $\R^{2n} = T^*\R^k\times\R^{2(n-k)}$. Set the coordinates of the space as follows.
$$(q_1,...,q_k,p_1,...,p_k) \in T^*\R^k\\
(x_1,y_1,...,x_{2(n-k)},y_{2(n-k)}) \in \R^{2(n-k)}$$
