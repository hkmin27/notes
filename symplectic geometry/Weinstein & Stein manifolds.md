### Weinstein manifolds

A symplectic manifold $(W,\omega)$ is called a *Weinstein manifold* if there are 

- a Morse function $\phi:W\to\R$ such that $\partial W$ is a regular level set.
- a gradient-like vector field $v$  for $\phi$
