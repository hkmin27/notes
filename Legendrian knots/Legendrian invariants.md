### LOSS invariant
Consider an oriented, null-homologous Legendrian knot $L \subset (Y,\xi)$. Then there is an open book decomposition  $(\Sigma,\phi)$ compatible with $(Y,\xi)$ such that $L$ sits on $\Sigma$ as a homologically essential curve.
